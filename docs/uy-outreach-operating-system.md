## Strategic filter

Be tasteful and tactical. The goal is not volume for volume's sake.

Primary outcomes:

1. Get the right people to sign up for and follow the Raleigh Metro magazine.
2. Identify trusted local businesses/providers that serve this community well and could reasonably afford visibility or sponsor options.

Use this classification on outreach and follow-up:

- `Magazine Signup Lead` — families, providers, nonprofits, schools, community connectors, and anyone who should receive/read/share the magazine.
- `Trusted Business Fit` — credible local businesses/providers families could actually use or trust.
- `Sponsor Option Fit` — trusted businesses with likely budget where a visibility/options conversation would be appropriate.
- `Story/Referral Lead` — people who may know a family, story, provider, business, or community voice worth surfacing.

Tone rules:

- Lead with usefulness to families.
- Be specific about why they stood out.
- For nonprofits/families/community connectors, prioritize signup, referrals, stories, and relationship-building.
- For trusted businesses with likely budget, softly open the door to options only after community fit is clear.
- Do not sound like generic ad sales.
- Do not pressure anyone into a sponsor conversation if they are better as a referral/source/reader.

## Daily lead batch

Automation: `UY Daily Lead Batch`

Schedule: weekdays at 10:20 AM ET.

Purpose: send a small, high-quality batch of new outreach each weekday.

Guardrails:

- Target 5-10 emails per weekday.
- Send fewer than 5 if fewer than 5 are genuinely strong.
- Search Gmail and Calendar before sending to avoid duplicate outreach or duplicate meetings.
- Every email must include a specific reason the lead stood out from a current/public source.
- No generic blasts.
- No `https://uyrdu.com/resources`.
- Use only `https://uyrdu.com` and `https://uyrdu.com/refer`.
- Keep tone warm, community-first, and non-pushy.
- For nonprofits/community connectors/family sources: prioritize signups, referrals, stories, and trust.
- For trusted businesses with likely budget: softly open sponsor/options only after community fit is clear.
- If verification or Gmail sending fails, do not guess; report the proposed batch instead.

Gmail label: `UY Daily Lead Batch`.
